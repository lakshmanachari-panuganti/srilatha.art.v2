# AI Prompt — Srilatha Art Customer Frontend (Features Only)

Build the **customer-facing frontend** for **Srilatha Art**, a handmade Indian folk-art store (resin art, Lippan art, dot mandala, Kolam art, wedding decoratives, gift items) that ships across India in INR.

This is a **features-only specification**. Do not assume any technology, framework, or architecture — just deliver a frontend that contains all of the following.

---

## 1. Brand & identity
- Logo and consistent brand presence across every page
- Warm, premium handcrafted aesthetic
- Footer with brand info, navigation, social links (Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook, YouTube), contact details

## 2. Homepage / landing
- Hero section
- Featured / curated collections showcase
- "Why choose Srilatha Art" / quality-promise section
- "How it's made" / handmade-process section
- About teaser
- Clear calls-to-action (shop, custom order, WhatsApp)

## 3. Artwork collections & browsing
- Browse all artwork
- Browse by category (Resin, Lippan, Dot Mandala, Kolam, Wedding Decor, Gifts)
- Curated views: Best Sellers, New Arrivals, Sale
- Filtering and sorting

## 4. Product pages
- Product detail with image gallery, description, material, care info, price
- Add to cart
- Add to wishlist
- Product reviews and ratings displayed
- Related / suggested products

## 5. Cart
- View cart, update quantities, remove items
- Cart totals (subtotal, shipping, discount, final)

## 6. Checkout
- Shipping address entry / saved addresses
- Delivery pincode serviceability check
- Apply coupon / discount code
- Order summary
- Online payment via Razorpay
- Order confirmation

## 7. Custom / bespoke orders
- Custom design order request flow
- Share idea, choose style/colors/dimensions, upload reference images
- Guided step explanation (idea → consultation → craft & delivery)

## 8. Customer accounts & login
- Customer sign-in and registration
- Login with Google
- Complete login management: password reset, forgot password, profile update
- Manage saved addresses

## 9. Account dashboard
- Profile overview
- Order history and single order detail
- Wishlist
- Download invoices

## 10. Order lifecycle (customer view)
- End-to-end order tracking: placed → confirmed → crafting → packed → shipped → out for delivery → delivered
- Cancel an order
- Request a return
- View refund status
- See on-hold / cancelled states

## 11. Reviews
- Submit a review after purchase
- Browse reviews / testimonials
- Star ratings

## 12. Offers & discounts
- Sale page with active coupons
- Coupon application
- Free-shipping threshold messaging

## 13. WhatsApp communication
- WhatsApp Business contact buttons throughout the site
- Customer receives WhatsApp notifications on each order activity (order placed, confirmed, shipped, delivered, etc.) via WhatsApp Cloud API

## 14. Email notifications
- Customer receives transactional emails for each order milestone and a post-delivery review request

## 15. Information & support pages
- About / Our Story
- The Craft
- Care Guide
- FAQ
- Shipping & Returns
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms

## 16. Engagement
- Newsletter subscription
- Site announcement banner
- Immersive process/storytelling film page

## 17. Quality of experience
- Fully mobile-responsive
- Accessible
- Fast-loading
- SEO-ready (meta tags, Open Graph, sitemap, indexable pages)
- Graceful loading, error, and "not found" states

---

**Note:** the admin panel is out of scope for this prompt — this covers the customer storefront only.
